CarFlexi is a broker agency operating in the field of tourism for many years offering multiple car rental solutions worldwide. Great offers for cheap car rental, daily, weekly long term rentals. Find the cheapest prices online, for your future Car Rental in 4 quick easy steps.

01 Problem

CarFlexi wants to update the design of the entire website, before that only updates/tests were performed on individual sections that did not improve the number of orders.

Task :Upgrade site https://www.carflexi.com/ The booking form is one of the most important elements of websites, but it is also important to highlight the pages of great car rental results.

02 Process

Competitors and pages providing similar services, as well as the most popular companies were analyzed.
Also, the existing website analysis, advantages, and disadvantages, based on analysis results the website was redesigned.

03 Result

By keeping the website's brand colors and the details, the website has been modernized.
